## 👋 About Me

Data Analyst with a background in software development, I work across the full data lifecycle, including data identification, collection, cleaning, statistical analysis, financial process automation, dynamic reporting and ETL workflows. I develop optimization solutions and organize business data using BI and cloud-based tools, and I structure operational workflows that support accurate and efficient decision-making. I'm organized and focused on improving processes through the practical application of technology and data.

In addition to technical knowledge, I bring interpersonal skills that contribute to effective collaboration in professional environments, including analytical reasoning, clear and objective communication, adaptability to new tools and contexts, attention to detail, teamwork, and a commitment to continuous learning, with a focus on delivering consistent and reliable contributions.
